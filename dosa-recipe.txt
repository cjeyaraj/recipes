Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup rice flour
 - 1 cup water
 - Pinch of salt
 - Yeast
 - finely chopped onions

Directions:
  1. Mix the rice flour in water and bring it to a thick consistency (batter). Add salt and Yeast
  2. Store the batter in a warm place near the fire place and wait for the yeast to ferment the flour
  3. Once fermented, mix the onions in the batter.
  4. Heat a tawa
  5. On the hot tawa, pour the batter into a flat circle.
  6. Wait till cooked. Turn around and lay the other side flat on the taw.
